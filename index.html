Why Grainwork

Crafted for people who care about their space

We obsess over every tenon joint and hand-rubbed oil finish so you can simply sit down and do your best thinking.

🪵
Solid Hardwood Only

No MDF. No particle board. Every surface you touch is genuine American White Oak, Walnut, or Cherry — sourced from FSC-certified forests.

🔨
Hand-Jointed Construction

Traditional mortise-and-tenon joints, not screws and cam locks. Our desks don't wobble after a year — they get better with age.

🌿
Sustainably Sourced

Every board is traced to a certified forest. We plant two trees for every one we use — because the grain of tomorrow matters.

✏️
Made to Measure

Send us your room dimensions and we'll size it perfectly. Corner configurations, cable management, and custom finishes available on request.

🛡️
15-Year Warranty

If a joint opens, a drawer sticks, or a finish fades in the first 15 years, we'll fix it. No forms, no fuss — a handshake promise.

📦
White-Glove Delivery

Your desk arrives fully assembled and placed exactly where you want it. Our installers protect your floors, test everything, and leave no dust behind.

Our Story

Built in a Vermont barn since 2008

Grainwork started when our founder, Thomas Aldric, couldn't find a desk worth keeping. So he built one — in his grandfather's timber-frame barn with hand tools and a secondhand bandsaw.

Fifteen years later, we still work in that same barn. We've added three more craftspeople, a few more saws, and over 4,200 desks shipped across North America. The ethos hasn't changed: every desk should outlast its owner.

We choose our wood the way a tailor chooses cloth — for character, for grain, for the way it will look 30 years from now.

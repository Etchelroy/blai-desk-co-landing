Why Grainwork

Built Different, From the Ground Up

Solid Hardwood Only

No MDF. No veneers. We use sustainably harvested white oak, walnut, and cherry — each plank chosen for character and structural integrity.

Heirloom Joinery

Mortise-and-tenon, dovetail, and drawbored pegs — traditional joinery that grows stronger with age and needs no hardware to hold.

Natural Oil Finish

We finish with food-safe Danish oil and beeswax — zero VOC, zero plastic coating. Your desk breathes, ages, and deepens in beauty over decades.

Our Story

Started With One Plank of Walnut

In 2000, James Okafor built his first desk in a rented Portland garage with hand tools his father brought from Lagos. Twenty-four years later, Grainwork is still family-run, still hand-tool first, and still obsessed with the quiet dignity of slow furniture.

We work in small batches — never more than 12 pieces at a time — so every desk gets the attention it deserves. Wood is sourced from Pacific Northwest sustainable forestry co-ops we've partnered with for over a decade.
